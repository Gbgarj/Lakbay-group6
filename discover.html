Experience Local Culture:

The various celebrations in the Philippines infuse enthusiasm into every month of the year with upbeat dances and vibrant feasts. The Filipino fiesta, with its parade floats, banderitas, and mouthwatering cuisine, has come to represent hospitality, giving, and a sense of community. We get to see the amazing cultural diversity that our nation has to offer, from Luzon to Mindanao.

Fiestas truly have roots in precolonial culture, as you may not have realized. According to reports from Antonio Pigafetta, Pedro Chirino, and Miguel de Loarca, the native Filipinos would already get together for feasts and offer thanks to the gods for a successful harvest before the Spaniards brought Catholicism to the nation.

Experience Riding on iconic Jeepney

The traditional Jeepney, with its flamboyant artwork and open sides, offers an immersive experience into the heart of Filipino culture. Roam through the bustling streets, feel the warm breeze, and engage with the friendly locals as you traverse the urban landscape in a ride that encapsulates the spirit of the Philippines.

From the lively markets to historical landmarks, the Jeepney is your ticket to an authentic and unforgettable adventure. So, don't miss the chance to embrace the rhythm of the streets, savor the distinct flavors of local life, and create memories that resonate with the soul of the Philippines. Hop on board the Jeepney, where every journey is a celebration of tradition and community!

Witness the majestic Philippine Eagle

Embark on a journey to witness the majestic Philippine Eagle, a symbol of both strength and conservation. Our guide invites you to explore the captivating world of this critically endangered species, found nowhere else but in the lush and pristine landscapes of the Philippines.

Head to the Philippine Eagle Center in Davao, where the echoes of the tropical rainforest mingle with the mesmerizing sight of these magnificent birds in their natural habitat. Marvel at their impressive wingspan and distinctive features as they soar through the skies with unmatched grace.

Step into the heart of Manila's past

The historical heart of Manila, where the echoes of the past resonate through cobblestone streets, centuries-old walls, and iconic landmarks. As you step into this walled city, you embark on a journey back in time, discovering the Spanish colonial legacy that has left an indelible mark on the Philippines.

Intramuros is not just a destination; it's a living testament to the Philippines' resilience, culture, and enduring spirit. Join us on a journey through time as you explore the captivating allure of Manila's historic gem. Your adventure in Intramuros awaits, where every step is a passage to a bygone era.
